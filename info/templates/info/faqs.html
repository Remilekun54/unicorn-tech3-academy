What is Unicorn-Tech3 Academy?

Unicorn-Tech3 Academy is an online tech-based primary and secondary school that teaches students from kindergarten to grade 12 in computer science, AI, and "street sense." The goal is to equip students with tech and entrepreneurial skills so they can build their own startups by the time they graduate.

Who can enroll in Unicorn-Tech3 Academy?

The academy is open to primary and secondary school students across Africa and beyond. We welcome students of all backgrounds who are eager to learn tech, AI, and entrepreneurship.

Is Unicorn-Tech3 Academy an accredited school?

We are working towards official accreditation and aligning our curriculum with international and African education standards. Our curriculum is modeled after Singapore, the UK, the USA, China, Florida, and Tennessee, while being tailored to African realities.
