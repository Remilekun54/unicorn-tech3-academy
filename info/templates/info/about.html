Why Choose Us

Why Unicorn-Tech3-Academy?

Unicorn-Tech3 Academy is more than a school – it’s a revolution in education, designed to inspire, equip, and transform young minds across Africa into world class tech innovators, entrepreneurs, and problem-solvers. Our vision is bold: to empower children from kindergarten to Grade 12 with cutting-edge skills in Artificial Intelligence, software development, and practical life skills – or what we call “Street Sense” – tailored to Africa’s unique realities.

At Unicorn-Tech3 Academy, we believe in unlocking every child’s potential to become a tech unicorn start-up founder – an innovator who not only thrives globally but also creates sustainable solutions to local challenges. By blending world-class technology education, ethical entrepreneurship, and cultural relevance, we’re nurturing a generation of thinkers, doers, and leaders who are ready to transform communities, industries, and the entire continent.

Our curriculum is carefully designed to spark creativity, foster collaboration, and inspire innovation. From hands-on coding and cybersecurity lessons to problem solving projects rooted in Africa’s diverse contexts, every aspect of our program is built to prepare students for the future. We’re not just teaching tech; we’re shaping the creators of tomorrow – young Africans who will launch start-ups, build impactful technologies, and contribute to solving critical challenges like poverty, unemployment, and access to quality education. At Unicorn-Tech3 Academy, we dare to dream big – and we empower our students to do the same. Let’s build a brighter, smarter, and more innovative Africa together. Welcome to the future of education. Welcome to Unicorn-Tech3 Academy!

What Is Our Vision & Mission ?

To transform Africa into a global hub of young tech innovators by equipping K-12 students with AI skills, software development/engineering expertise, and practical life skills tailored to the continent’s realities, empowering them to launch startups, solve local challenges, and drive sustainable development.

To provide African primary and secondary school students with a world-class technology education rooted in practical skills and cultural relevance, enabling them to innovate, create jobs, and tackle critical issues like poverty, unemployment, and access to quality education.
